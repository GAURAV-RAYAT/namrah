Security Audits

Provision of security is, in all likelihood, an important part of your business - and you sure are investing quite a bit in this. Whilst security companies do provide the best they can, it is always a good idea to have a 3rd party professional complete a security audit in Delhi on how your system is working. In order to ensure that you are getting the most value out of your investment in security, we can audit and suggest alternative or better ways of doing the same work.

Our security audits in Gurgaon and beyond include, but are not limited to:
Evaluate the training standards, gaps and requirements.
Review of the documents, forms, security reports and logs maintained.
Efficacy of existing SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) and steps to imporve.
Need for Staff training for generating synergy.
Risks, Vulnerabilities and Threat assessment.
Evaluation report with Gaps in security apparatus and recommendations for efficient functioning of security organisation.
Developing and documenting procedures, creating forms, logs, establishing security systems.
Need for electronic surveillance systems.
